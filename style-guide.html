Style Guide

Selected Fonts:

The fonts are simple with title fonts and paragraph fonts. For paragraph fonts such as this one. The site will use Source Sans Pro. A San-sarif font that should be easy to read with minimal line height.

For title fonts, the site will use Raleway Master, obtained from League of moveable type. The font is thin, with extra curves on the ending line. Even with larger size it is easy to read.
